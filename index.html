Sheeks About Shetland — Episode 1

Jane and Pete introduce the first episode of Sheeks About Shetland.

Jane is a seasoned broadcaster with BBC Radio Shetland while Pete has reported for The Shetland Times, BBC Radio Shetland and Shetland News. The pair feel there is a gap in the islands' media landscape for longer conversations unrestricted by the time and space limitations of other print and broadcast media.
